# Contributing

## AI Collaboration

This repository is produced through collaboration between human contributors and AI tools.
All AI-assisted outputs should be reviewed, validated, and curated by maintainers before inclusion.

## Licensing of Contributions

By submitting a contribution to ByteTaper, you agree that your contribution is
licensed under the same dual-license terms as the project:

```text
AGPL-3.0-only OR LicenseRef-Commercial
```

This means the project maintainer may make your accepted contribution available
under AGPL-3.0-only and may also include it in commercial ByteTaper builds under
separate commercial license agreements.

Do not submit code, documentation, generated files, `.proto` definitions, test
fixtures, vendored source, or other materials unless you have the right to
contribute them under these terms.

## Third-Party Materials

If a contribution adds, vendors, copies, generates from, or links to third-party
materials, include the relevant license and notice updates in the same pull
request. At minimum, update:

- `THIRD_PARTY_NOTICES.md`
- `LICENSES/` when a copied license text is required
- source headers or file-level notices when required by the upstream license

Do not replace upstream copyright or license headers with ByteTaper headers.
Preserve the upstream notices and add modification notes where appropriate.

## Developer Certificate of Origin

For now, ByteTaper expects contributors to certify that they have the right to
submit their contribution under the project license terms. A future contributor
license agreement may be introduced before accepting broad external
contributions.
